MBTI Personality Test

Explore Your Personality Type

The Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI) is a personality assessment based on Carl Jung's theory of psychological types. Your test results will show your preferences across four dimensions:

Extraversion (E) vs. Introversion (I)

Do you prefer to draw energy from the outer world or your inner world?

Sensing (S) vs. Intuition (N)

Do you focus more on concrete facts or possibilities and patterns?

Thinking (T) vs. Feeling (F)

Do you make decisions based on logic or personal values?

Judging (J) vs. Perceiving (P)

Do you prefer a planned lifestyle or staying flexible and adaptable?

Test Instructions:

  • This test consists of 20 questions, each corresponding to a dimension trait
  • There are no right or wrong answers - respond based on your true feelings
  • Choose the option that best reflects your typical behavior and thinking
  • After completion, you'll see your MBTI type with detailed analysis
